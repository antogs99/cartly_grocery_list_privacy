# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Antonio Gutierrez ("we", "us", or "our") built Cartly as a commercial app. 
This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and 
disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and 
display name. This is used solely to identify your account and personalize 
your experience.

**List and Item Data**
Your grocery lists, items, and shopping history are stored in Google 
Firestore under your account. This data is used to sync your lists across 
devices and enable list sharing.

**Usage Analytics**
We collect anonymous usage events (such as features tapped and flows 
completed) to understand how the app is used and improve it. This data is 
collected via Firebase Analytics and is not linked to your personal identity.

**Subscription Data**
Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store 
your payment information. We store your subscription entitlement status in 
Firestore to grant access to paid features.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app
- To sync your data across your devices
- To enable list sharing with other users you invite
- To process and verify subscription access
- To improve the app based on usage patterns

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Google Firebase** — for authentication, database storage, and analytics
- **Apple** — for subscription processing and Sign in with Apple

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your 
account at any time from the app's Settings screen, which permanently 
deletes all your lists, items, and account information.

## Children's Privacy

Cartly is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect 
personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes 
by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
